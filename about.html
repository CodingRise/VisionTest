Welcome to our eye testing app, designed to help you assess and improve your visual acuity. Our app offers a comprehensive eye testing experience with 15 levels of increasing complexity. With each level, you'll be presented with a random text to read and input. The difficulty gradually decreases as you progress through the levels, ensuring a challenging yet manageable experience for users of all skill levels. Once you've completed all 15 levels, our app provides you with a detailed eye performance report. This report offers insights into your visual accuracy and speed, allowing you to track your progress and identify areas for improvement. Whether you're looking to monitor your vision or simply enjoy a fun and interactive way to test your eyes, our eye testing app is here to help. Start your journey to better visual health today!
